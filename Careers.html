TATTOO ARTIST

Becoming a tattoo artist is the most direct path to a career in the tattoo industry. You'll need to develop your artistic skills, learn about different tattoo styles, and gain experience in tattooing. Most tattoo artists work in tattoo shops, where they create custom designs or apply existing ones to clients. It's essential to get proper training and, in many places, a license to become a tattoo artist.

TATTOO DESIGNER

If you have strong design skills but don't want to perform actual tattooing, you can work as a tattoo designer. Tattoo designers create custom tattoo designs for clients, which can be handed over to tattoo artists for application. Tattoo Equipment Supplier: You can start a business selling tattoo equipment and supplies to tattoo artists and shops. This includes tattoo machines, ink, needles, and other related products.!!.
